CodexBar Usage Monitor keeps your AI usage, limits, balances, and refresh status in one focused iPhone and iPad dashboard.

Built for people who move between multiple AI coding and assistant tools, CodexBar helps you check what is available before starting work.

Supported providers:

- ChatGPT / Codex
- GitHub Copilot
- Claude
- Cursor
- OpenRouter
- OpenCode ZEN

What you can track:

- Usage limits, balance details, reset timing, refresh status, and alerts where each provider makes those metrics available
- Multiple accounts and grouped provider configurations, so work, personal, and team accounts can stay organized
- Recent history and trend context for configured accounts
- Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets with configurable tiles for the providers you care about most

Privacy and account model:

- CodexBar does not require a CodexBar account
- CodexBar does not send your provider credentials or usage data to a HemSoft backend
- Credentials are stored locally on your device using Keychain where appropriate
- Usage and balance requests are made directly from your device to the providers you configure

CodexBar is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the providers it can display. Provider names are used only to identify the services you choose to connect.
